PADANG!

The heart of West Sumatra. Located near the west coastline, Padang is the capital city of West Sumatra. It is the 16th most populous city in Indonesia and the most populous city on the west coast of Sumatra. Padang is widely known for its Minangkabau culture, cuisine, and sunset beaches.
