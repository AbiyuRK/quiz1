FOOD

RENDANG

Rendang is a rich dish of meat that has been slow cooked and braised in a coconut milk seasoned with a herb and spice mixture over a period of several hours until the liquids evaporate and the meat turns dark brown and tender, becoming caramelized and infused with rich flavours. It originated from the Minangkabau region in West Sumatra, Indonesia.
